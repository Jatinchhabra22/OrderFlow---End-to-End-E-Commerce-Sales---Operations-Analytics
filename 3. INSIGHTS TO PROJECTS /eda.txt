Key Insights from EDA:

1. Majority of revenue is generated from a small number of product categories
2. Certain sizes consistently outperform others in sales volume
3. Order success rate varies significantly by fulfilment type
4. Metro zones contribute higher revenue but also higher failure rates
5. Month-wise sales show clear seasonality trends
6. Failed orders are concentrated in specific regions
7. High-priced items have lower order volume but higher revenue contribution
8. Certain courier statuses strongly correlate with delivery failures
9. Zone-wise analysis reveals logistics inefficiencies
10. Order success rate improves for mid-range price segments
