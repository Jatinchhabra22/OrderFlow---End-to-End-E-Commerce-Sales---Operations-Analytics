Features Created:

1. Sales Column:
   - Converts raw amount into business revenue metric

2. Success Flag:
   - Binary indicator for successful vs failed orders

3. Price_Segment:
   - Low / Medium / High pricing classification
   - Helps in pricing strategy analysis

4. Size_Group:
   - Aggregated size categories for better insights

5. Zone:
   - Regional grouping for logistics analysis

6. Date Breakdown:
   - Year, Month, Day extracted for time series analysis

Business Value:
- Enables stakeholders and businesses help visualise their sales and dataset
- Enables KPI creation
- Supports executive dashboards
- Simplifies SQL and BI analysis
